IT Ethics: Guidelines for Developers

Ethical behavior in information technology ensures that systems are secure, fair, and respectful of user rights. Below are key principles recognized by major professional organizations.

1. Protect Privacy

Developers must design systems that safeguard personal data and minimize unnecessary data collection.

2. Ensure Security

Systems should be built with strong security controls to prevent unauthorized access and misuse.

3. Avoid Harm

IT professionals must avoid creating technologies that cause physical, psychological, or social harm.

4. Be Transparent

Users should be informed about how their data is used and how automated systems make decisions.
